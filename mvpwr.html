BEFORE YOU DIAL — You have the quote ready. Call within 2 minutes.

Opening

"Hi, is this Name? Hey, this is Jesse over at Mountain View Pet Waste Removal — I just got your request come through and wanted to give you a quick call. Did I catch you at an okay time?"

Transition

"Perfect. So I saw you've got X dogs over in Town — sounds like you're ready to get the yard sorted out. Is this just winter buildup you're dealing with, or has it been a little longer than that?"
If a few months: "Yeah, that's exactly what we're seeing all over right now. Spring hits and it's a lot."

If longer: "Okay, good to know — that actually works in your favor with what I've got going on right now."

The Anchor

"So let me tell you what I've got for you. First — if you just needed a one-time spring cleanup, no recurring service, that would run you $___. That's us coming out, clearing out everything that's built up, leaving the yard completely clean. Done."
Pause. Let it land.

The Offer

"But here's what I'm running right now through the spring. If you want to keep it clean going forward — we come every week, you never think about it again — I can knock that initial cleanup down to $___. Same full cleanup, just discounted because you're starting recurring service.

Your first regular weekly visit after the cleanup is on me, and we'll throw in a complimentary yard deodorizer when we're out there.

Weekly service after that is $___/month — that's about $___ per cleanup. And it comes with a 30-day guarantee — if you're not happy for any reason in that first month, I'll refund that month of service. No hassle."
Pause. Don't fill the silence.

Soft Close

"Does that sound like something that would work for you?"

Objections

"Totally get it. Can I ask — is it more a question of whether to do it, or more about the timing and the price?"
If just checking in: "That's fair. I can hold this Spring Special pricing for you for 48 hours. After that I can't guarantee the discounted cleanup rate. Want me to shoot you a text with everything so you've got it in front of you when you chat with them?"

If not sure they need it: "That's fair too. The guarantee means there's really no risk in trying it — worst case, it cost you nothing and your yard got cleaned."
After hanging up: Start Sequence C in the tracker.
"I hear you. If weekly feels like too much right now, I do offer bi-weekly service as well — every other week, $___/month. Same setup, just a little less frequent. Want me to run that number for you?"
Downsell order: Weekly first, bi-weekly if they push back on price.
"No problem. I'll shoot you a text with everything. Just so you know, the Spring Special pricing is only good for 48 hours — after that the cleanup goes back to full price. I'll follow up in a day or two."
After hanging up: Start Sequence B in the tracker immediately.
"You're in Town — yes, we're out that way! Let me get you set up."
"I bill the cleanup after we do the job — so you don't pay until it's done. Then I pre-bill monthly service on the first of the month. If we're starting mid-month, I'll pro-rate that first billing so you're only paying for the days we're serving you."

The Close

"So — you want to get you on the schedule?"
